About Me

My journey began in Columbia, South Carolina, where Southern storytelling shaped my creative lens. Now, as a Senior Connections Strategist at TBWA\Chiat\Day in Los Angeles, I bring a global mindset to my work, honed at agencies like Wieden + Kennedy and Goodby, Silverstein & Partners.

A University of South Carolina grad (B.A. Mass Communications, 2020), I'm driven by curiosity, community, DE&I, and cutting-edge tech like AI and Web3. My work has earned a 2024 Cannes Titanium Grand Prix, but my mission is connecting people, ideas, and culturesalways with a story worth telling.

Role Lead Strategic Planner
Agency TBWA\Chiat\Day
Year 2024
Scroll to explore

Campaign Overview

The Levis REIIMAGINE campaign with Beyonc is a bold celebration of the brands legacy as a canvas for self-expression, reimagining iconic ads for a modern era. Inspired by Beyoncs track LEVIIS JEANS from her critically acclaimed album COWBOY CARTER, the campaign unfolds in chapters, each reinterpreting a classic Levis advertisement. I led the strategy at TBWA\Chiat\Day, focusing on connecting with fans through a global, multi-channel approach. The campaign sparked $5M+ in earned media within 48 hours, proving Levis remains the definitive denim lifestyle brand.

Brand Work / Samuel Adams "Brighter Boston"

Samuel Adams "Your Cousins Brighter Boston" Campaign

AgencyGoodby, Silverstein & Partners
Year2023
Scroll to explore

In the "Your Cousins Brighter Boston" campaign for Samuel Adams, I helped bring a fresh perspective to the iconic Boston beer brand at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners. As a lifelong Celtics fan, I pitched partnering with NBA legend Kevin Garnett, whose energy perfectly embodied Boston's spirit. Directed by Aaron Stoller, the campaign infused visuals with a bold, cinematic flair, celebrating Bostons heritage and community. Launched during Super Bowl LVII in 2023, the spot aired in 15 markets and was supported by a robust media plan across TV, digital, and OOH.
